# Situation
Meaning-laden work keeps losing to frictionless shallow tasks; days feel busy but low-output.

# Approaches Tried
Scheduling a fixed daily deep-work block; removing the phone from the room; batching shallow work to the afternoon.

# What Worked
Protecting one morning block with the trigger removed (phone elsewhere) reliably produced focused output.

# What Didn't
Relying on willpower in the moment while keeping distractions in reach.

# Lesson / Principle
Design the environment to remove the trigger; don't fight the urge in real time.

# Source & Attribution
Cal Newport, *Deep Work*; behavioral-design practice.
