# Situation
Irregular sleep timing causes sleep debt, circadian drift, and daytime symptoms like headaches.

# Approaches Tried
Fixing a single wake time every day; a 60-minute screens-off wind-down; cutting afternoon caffeine; hydrating on waking; a 7-day sleep log.

# What Worked
A fixed wake time (even weekends) is the strongest anchor; cutting caffeine after early afternoon and a screen curfew improved sleep quality within ~1-2 weeks.

# What Didn't
Sleeping in or napping to "catch up" after a bad night prolonged the drift.

# Lesson / Principle
Anchor the wake time first; the rest of the routine follows. Fix the system, not the symptom.
